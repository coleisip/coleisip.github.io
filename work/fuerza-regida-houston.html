The Challenge

Fuerza Regida had one of the most culturally significant years in regional Mexican music — and Dos Equis had a partnership with them. The opportunity was to turn that partnership into something more than a logo placement: a brand-owned cultural moment in a key market.

The Role

Account lead for a proprietary concert activation in Houston — one of only three U.S. performances during the band's historic year. Responsible for concepting the brand environment, overseeing production, and managing the PR and content strategy built around the night.

Responsibilities
Account Lead Brand Environment Design Content Strategy PR Coordination Market Activation
Execution

The brief wasn't to show up at a Fuerza Regida concert — it was to build a moment that belonged to Dos Equis. We constructed a brand environment from the ground up: not a logo wall, but a fully designed space engineered to generate the photography and footage that would carry the campaign beyond the night itself.

Every visual element was built for distribution — from the lighting design to the signage to the talent positioning — because the real audience wasn't in the room. It was the readers of Complex, Hypebeast, and Billboard Latin who would encounter the brand through editorial coverage the following week.

To extend reach beyond a single market, we partnered with Complex to host watch parties across three additional Texas markets — turning one night in Houston into a multi-city activation that amplified both the concert and the brand's connection to the moment.
